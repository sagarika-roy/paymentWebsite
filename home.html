Stars on my mind

It is three at night, trying my best to sleep, but my mind is somewhere away from sleep, and this gust of wind peeking through my open window makes my mind up to go up and enjoy the rusty feeling of the night. I get to my terrace on tiptoeing, clear night along with the bise calms me. There is always something secretive about the night; the presence of stars makes the night mysterious and heavenly beautiful. Ancient people always said that the more beautiful element of the night are the stars; the stars are the trinket of the night ''the glistening dome'', the source of luminous night. Ancestors also said that after death soul became a star. It is estimated that; there are two trillion or two billion galaxies in the universe, among which The Milky way, is just one of a mere 100 billion galaxies in the universe made of four hundred billion stars along with dead stars which had already completed their life cycle and also the interstellar medium(ISM), The ISM is the space between the stars, it is the birthplace of a star indeed;

I just literally lost myself under the stars, grieving to visit those deity's art in nature or of nature; every time I look high, my soul every time feels bliss.
